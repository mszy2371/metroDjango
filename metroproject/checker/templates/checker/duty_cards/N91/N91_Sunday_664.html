Routes N20 & N91 are LOW FREQUENCY - drivers must ensure that they DO NOT DEPART EARLY from ANY timing point.
The starting point for delivering a good service is for every bus to depart from each terminus ON TIME. If you cannot depart on time, advise iBus control as to why.






































In the event of late running, drivers MUST check that driving hours are legal: Maximum spell of driving without a break (EXCLUDING STAND TIME) = 5 hours 30 minutes - after which a 30 minute break MUST be taken UNLESS total stand time already taken is more than 45 minutes. The daily maximum DRIVING limit is 10 hours 00 minutes. Always record your stand times, and deduct these from your total driving times.



















IF YOU BELIEVE THAT YOU MAY EXCEED ANY OF THESE LIMITS, CONTACT IBUS CONTROL AS SOON AS POSSIBLE AND FOLLOW THEIR INSTRUCTIONS.
